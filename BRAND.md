# Aqualyx Leeds Brand Tokens

## Primary
- `--primary`: Light blue clinical action color
- `--primary-foreground`: White text on primary surfaces

## Secondary
- `--secondary`: Deep navy/charcoal content accent
- `--secondary-foreground`: White for secondary buttons/chips

## Neutrals
- `--background`: Soft medical white-blue background
- `--card`: Elevated white surface
- `--muted`: Light neutral section backgrounds
- `--border`: Soft neutral borders

## Button Styles
- Primary CTA: solid primary, medium radius, strong weight
- Secondary CTA: outline secondary foreground on white
- Utility CTA: muted ghost for tertiary actions

## Card Styles
- White card on neutral background
- 1px neutral border + soft shadow
- Heading + concise benefit copy + direct CTA

## Icon Usage
- Line icons only (Lucide)
- Use icons for trust points, process steps, and contact actions
- Keep icon color to primary/secondary tokens only
