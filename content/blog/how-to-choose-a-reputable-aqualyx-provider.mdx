## Why provider choice matters

The same treatment name can be delivered very differently across clinics. Your result and experience often depend more on provider process than on marketing claims.

## Reputable provider checklist

- Consultation-first, not sales-first
- Clear discussion of suitability and contraindications
- Transparent pricing logic
- Structured aftercare and review pathway
- Honest language about variable outcomes

## Compare clinics with this table

| Trust factor | What good looks like | Red flag |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Consultation | Detailed screening and goal mapping | Rushed or skipped |
| Pricing | Clear per-ml or package explanation | Hidden add-ons |
| Claims | “Results vary, consultation required” | “Guaranteed results” |
| Follow-up | Defined review and support | No review plan |

## Questions worth asking before booking

- Who assesses suitability?
- How do you decide dosage/session plan?
- What outcomes are realistic for my area?
- What should I expect after treatment?
- When should I contact you urgently?

## Why transparent communication wins

A trustworthy clinic will explain limitations, not just benefits. That includes:

- Variable timelines
- Need for staged sessions in some cases
- Uncertainty of individual response
