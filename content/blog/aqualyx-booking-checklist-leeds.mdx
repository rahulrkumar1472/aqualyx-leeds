## Why preparation improves your consultation

A prepared booking helps the clinic team respond faster and more accurately. It also helps you get practical answers on suitability, price range, and timeline from the first conversation.

## Booking form checklist

- Full name and best contact number
- Email for confirmations
- Target area (chin, stomach, love handles, arms, thighs, etc.)
- Preferred contact method (WhatsApp, call, or email)
- Preferred date/time if you have one
- Optional notes about your goal

## What the clinic usually asks next

| Question | Why it is asked |
| --- | --- |
| Which area concerns you most? | Focuses consultation planning |
| What timeline do you have in mind? | Sets realistic expectation windows |
| Have you had similar treatment before? | Helps safety and planning context |

## Before you submit

- Check your phone number is correct
- Add any important scheduling notes
- Confirm you are happy to be contacted
