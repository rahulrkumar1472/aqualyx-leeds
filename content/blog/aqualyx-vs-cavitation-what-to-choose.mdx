## Why this comparison comes up often

Clients often want non-injection alternatives while still targeting stubborn areas. Cavitation is commonly discussed in that context.

## Core differences

| Topic | Aqualyx | Cavitation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Method | Injection pathway | Ultrasound session pathway |
| Typical planning | Dosage by area | Session package by area |
| Use case | Localised contouring goals | Non-invasive contour support |
| Review approach | Staged review | Session progression review |

## How to choose safely

Prioritise this order:

1. Suitability and contraindication screening
2. Anatomical fit for the target area
3. Realistic timeline and review plan
4. Budget and session commitment

## Cost logic comparison
