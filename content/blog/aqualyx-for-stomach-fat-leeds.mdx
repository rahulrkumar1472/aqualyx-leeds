## What people usually mean by “stomach fat treatment”

Most people are talking about localised lower-abdominal fat pockets that remain despite lifestyle effort. Aqualyx planning in this context is a contouring pathway, not a total weight-loss programme.

## Why stomach-area planning is different

Compared with smaller zones like the chin, abdominal areas often need:

- Wider treatment mapping
- Careful dosage planning by sub-zone
- Staged scheduling and review checkpoints

This is why consultation quality matters.

## Typical consultation checklist for abdomen cases

- Is the area suitable for fat dissolving treatment?
- Which sub-zones are realistic to target first?
- What is the typical ml discussion range by zone?
- How many staged sessions might be discussed?
- What outcomes are realistic for your starting point?

## Timeline expectations table

| Timeline window | Common discussion points |
| --- | --- |
| Before treatment | Suitability, area mapping, cost estimate |
| Week 1 | Recovery awareness and aftercare adherence |
| Weeks 2-6 | Gradual contour changes for some clients |
| Review stage | Reassessment and possible additional session planning |

No clinic can responsibly promise identical timing for everyone.

## Suitability and safety notes

A responsible pathway should include:

- Medical screening and contraindication review
- Practical conversation about expectations and limitations
- Clear instruction on when to contact the clinic

You should postpone treatment if suitability criteria are not met.

## Cost and value discussion

The total cost depends on:
